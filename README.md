# Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior to identify key drivers of attrition and estimate business revenue impact. The goal is to provide actionable insights to improve customer retention and lifetime value.

## Business Questions
- What factors contribute most to customer churn?
- Which customer segments are at the highest risk?
- How much revenue is lost due to churn?
- What retention strategies can reduce churn?

## Key Insights
- Identified high-risk churn segments based on tenure, service usage, and contract type
- Estimated churn rate and associated revenue loss
- Analyzed customer behavior patterns to highlight retention opportunities
- Generated business recommendations to improve customer lifetime value (CLV)

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Jupyter Notebook
- PowerBi
- Excel

## Outcome
Delivered actionable insights to support customer retention strategy, marketing optimization, and revenue protection.
